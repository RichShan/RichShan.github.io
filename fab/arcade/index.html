Tiny Arcade

Today, I fully built and assembled the Tiny Arcade kit. The kit consisted of a bunch of small plastic parts which would be assembled together to create a small arcade machine. The hardest part was pushing the plastic pieces together as the acrylic was pretty roughly cut. I solved this through constant tinkering and trying different angles. The build had bad structural integrity due to only being held together by a single screw and would fall apart during the assembly process. After all the pieces had been put in place, the arcade machine was stable and would stand on its own.
